Our Mission

At Retail Well Spring, we are dedicated to providing comprehensive retail auditing services that help brands identify inefficiencies, optimize inventory, and reduce food waste. One of the key tools in this effort is the Retail Inventory Tracker, a mobile-first application built using Expo (React Native) and Supabase. This tool facilitates the field audits by allowing users to create and view audit submissions, attach photos, and download and share the data.

The Retail Inventory Tracker helps streamline the process of data collection and management, playing a crucial role in reducing food waste by ensuring more accurate and efficient audits.
